I'm Avinash Sharma, a UI/UX Designer based in Punjab, India. With over 3 years of professional experience, I specialize in crafting scalable web and mobile products that balance user needs with business goals.

My work spans fintech, B2B platforms, and social impact applications — from redesigning federal banking payment workflows to creating platforms that help underserved communities access vital resources. I bring a data-driven, user-centric approach to every project.

I'm also passionate about integrating AI into the design workflow, using tools like Claude Code and Figma MCP to bridge the gap between design and development, enabling rapid prototyping and faster iteration cycles.
